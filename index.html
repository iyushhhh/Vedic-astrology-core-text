The Core Texts of Vedic Astrology

Explore the foundational scriptures that form the backbone of Jyotisha Shastra

Vedic astrology, known as Jyotisha, is an ancient system of astronomical and astrological knowledge that has guided humanity for millennia. The wisdom contained within these core texts provides profound insights into the cosmic influences that shape our lives and destinies.

Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra

The most comprehensive foundation text with 97 chapters covering all aspects of predictive astrology.

Jaimini Sutram

Unique system with variable significators and advanced timing techniques in 936 sutras.

Brihat Jataka

Standard textbook by Varahamihira with fundamental principles in 407 verses.

Garga Samhita

Contains mundane astrology principles alongside Krishna's divine cosmic significance.

Brihat Samhita

Comprehensive encyclopedia of mundane astrology with 107 chapters on various topics.

Unique Contributions of Each Text

Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra

  • Most comprehensive treatment of divisional charts
  • Detailed planetary strength calculations
  • Extensive yoga combinations
  • Longevity calculation methods

Jaimini Sutram

  • Revolutionary Chara Karaka system
  • Rasi aspects instead of graha aspects
  • Arudha Pada concepts
  • Advanced timing through Char Dasha

Brihat Jataka

  • Systematic Ashtakavarga method
  • Pancha Mahapurusha yogas
  • Female horoscopy principles
  • Lost horoscope reconstruction

Garga Samhita

  • Integration of spirituality and astrology
  • Agricultural timing principles
  • Vedic calendar systems
  • Cosmic significance of divine incarnations

Brihat Samhita

  • Comprehensive weather prediction
  • Architectural astrology (Vastu)
  • Gemology and remedial measures
  • Political and social astrology

Study Guide & Recommendations

Beginner Level

Recommended Starting Point

  • Begin with Brihat Jataka for fundamental concepts
  • Study chapters 1-25 of Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra
  • Focus on basic planetary meanings and sign characteristics
  • Understand the concept of benefics and malefics
  • Learn the twelve houses and their significations
Estimated Duration: 6-12 months

Intermediate Level

Deepening Understanding

  • Complete study of Brihat Parashara Hora Shastra
  • Introduction to Jaimini concepts - Chara Karakas and Arudhas
  • Master divisional charts (Vargas) and their applications
  • Study yoga combinations and their effects
  • Learn Ashtakavarga system from Brihat Jataka
  • Understand basic dasha calculations
Estimated Duration: 1-2 years

Advanced Level

Mastery and Integration

  • Master all five core texts thoroughly
  • Compare and integrate different systems and approaches
  • Study mundane astrology from Brihat Samhita
  • Apply advanced Jaimini techniques
  • Learn remedial measures and their proper application
  • Practice synthesis of multiple chart analysis methods
Estimated Duration: 3-5 years
